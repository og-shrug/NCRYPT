SEEDHE MAUT

Seedhe Maut is a hip hop duo from New Delhi, India, consisting of Siddhant Sharma and Abhijay Negi.

The two met at Spit Dope Inc., a Delhi underground battle rap league organised by MC Kode and Encore ABJ. They released their first track, Seedhe Maut Anthem in May 2017, followed by their debut mixtape 2 Ka Pahada
