SEEDHE MAUT

Seedhe Maut is a talented duo band that has made waves in the Indian hip-hop scene. Comprised of rappers Encore ABJ and Calm, they blend traditional desi beats with contemporary rap, creating a unique sound. With their razor-sharp lyrics and infectious energy, Seedhe Maut has gained a dedicated fan base and critical acclaim.

Their debut EP "Bayaan" showcased their lyrical prowess and storytelling abilities, with tracks like "101" and "Kyun" becoming fan favorites. Their talent and passion make them a force to be reckoned with in the world of hip-hop.
